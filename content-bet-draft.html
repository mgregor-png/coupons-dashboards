What is this Bet?

The content team operates at the intersection of offer pipelines, merchant management, and SEO - yet most of their critical workflows rely on manual processes, fragmented tooling, and workarounds that don't scale. Missing codes are not flowing into the pending queue. Bulk uploads still require manual CSV wrangling across territories. Tier updates for 1,000+ merchants happen through individual edits. Meanwhile, engineering has signaled that tickets without approved bets will not receive capacity starting Q2.

This bet creates a bounded, outcome-driven initiative to automate and improve the content pipeline - from offer ingestion through quality management to operational workflows - and resolve the SEO technical debt and CC tooling gaps that limit the content team's effectiveness. It covers 5 workstreams across content automation, SEO, and CC platform improvements.

Hypothesis

If we automate the content pipeline (missing codes ingestion, bulk offer generation, tier management), enable programmatic access to Control Cloud via MCP, and resolve SEO technical debt - then we will increase offer coverage per merchant, reduce manual content operations effort, and improve content freshness and organic traffic.

Key Assumptions

  • Scaling missing codes ingestion from 500 to 1,500 merchants captures offers we're currently losing to competitors
  • AI-powered bulk offer generation maintains quality at 10x scale, keeping minimum offer thresholds for SEO
  • Control Cloud MCP unlocks automation workflows that currently require manual UI interaction
  • Unresolved SEO and CC platform issues are silently degrading performance of strategic bets
