The Connective Tissue

Groupon Bucks - Why Users Come Back

Every interaction connects through one mechanism - a visible, growing balance that gives users a reason to come back even when they're not shopping.

The earn/spend loop: coupon use, cashback, code verifications, referrals - all earn Bucks. Bucks get spent on core Groupon deals. One currency that bridges the coupon web world and the deals app world.

"$14.20 earned this month"

Users check their balance even when they're not shopping. That's the habit loop.

Shop on Nike → Groupon overlay → Earn Bucks → Open app to redeem → Book local deal → Earn more Bucks
Engagement Hooks

What Keeps Users Coming Back

1

Coupon auto-apply

2

Cashback in Bucks

Answering the Brief

How This Delivers on Dusan's Four Priorities

Double monetisation

Open app more frequently

3+ weekly touchpoints vs current 1.5x/month. Brand following, Bucks balance checks, weekly digest all pull users into the app.

Every Safari interaction bridges back to the app via Bucks redemption. Web users become app users through the earn/spend loop.

Improve retention & frequency

Bucks 90-day expiry drives urgency. Brand following creates habitual check-ins. Cashback rewards repeat behaviour.
