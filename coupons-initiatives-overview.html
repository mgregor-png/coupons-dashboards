Why cashback is a content lever, not just a monetisation path

Today, coupons pages only have content for merchants that provide promo codes. Cashback lets us create an offer for any retailer - even those with zero existing content. This generates new impressions across both web (coupons pages) and extensions (Genie + Safari).

More merchants with offers means more pages with content, more reasons for users to visit, and more activation surface for the extensions. Cashback feeds every other path in the tree.
