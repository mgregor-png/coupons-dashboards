Cashback as a growth lever - 4 goals

1. User acquisition - gate cashback behind registration to turn anonymous traffic into Groupon users. 2. App upsell - position the app as the cashback control center (tracking, notifications). 3. Retention - cashback triggers bring users back to the app. 4. Deal conversion - convert cashback into Groupon Bucks, cross-sell deals.

Core funnel: Anonymous coupon traffic -> Registered user -> App user -> First deal -> Repeat

Also creates content for any retailer (even without promo codes), generating impressions across web, Genie, and Safari. Drives SEO defense through more pages with real offers.

Funnel estimate (from full deck, 95M all sessions): 25% reg rate -> 23M registrations -> 5% cashback txns (1.1M) -> 50% Bucks conversion -> 2.8M annual Groupon purchases. Coupons organic is ~30M of those sessions.

PoC scope: ~20 MD internal with AI-first team (merchant page 4MD, offer type 2MD, registration 1MD, profile 5MD, Bucks conversion 2MD, withdrawal 1MD + coordination). External dependencies: Core B2C registration, Bloomreach emails, Financial dept for Bucks. Full deck ↗

Three layers

Each builds on the previous. Not mutually exclusive - they stack.

Layer 1 - Quick wins
01 Monetise
On-page monetisation of non-monetised sessions: display ads (~$277K/yr, 2-3 MD), deal cross-sell (~$241-417K/yr, 5 MD), Genie upsell CTA (1 MD). Pure revenue extraction from existing 30M sessions. No registration needed. ~8 MD total.
Layer 2 - Genie + Rewards + Registration
Highest conviction
02 Register + 03 Genie rewards + 03 Genie deals
Extension users are the most valuable audience in the entire ecosystem. Cross-channel data shows extension buyers are 6x more likely than web buyers to make 3+ purchases/yr, and Rewards participation delivers the highest lift of any channel (+110% revenue/user on extensions vs +41% on web). Registration (~15 MD) connects coupons to Groupon core identity via GraphQL, gates rewards behind account creation, and enables delayed registration via email (Bloomreach + Core B2C deps). This is the identity layer both Genie and cashback need downstream - 540K regs/yr at $3.83 LTV = ~$2.1M/yr.
