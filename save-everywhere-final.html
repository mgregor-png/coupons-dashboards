Save Everywhere with Groupon

How coupons content captures the Groupon app userbase off-platform and through Groupon Bucks pulls them into the Groupon app

The Big Idea

Coupons content provides a mechanism for Groupon to be part of our customers' shopping experience across thousands of brands and millions of checkouts.

By delivering coupons content through a Safari extension within the Groupon app, we can extend Groupon's reach to deliver value for our customers across all their shopping experiences.

This captures users off-platform and through Groupon Bucks brings them back into the Groupon app.

How It Works

1

A Safari extension is added to the Groupon app. When a user downloads the app and enables the extension, Groupon will pop up when they shop at leading retailers on their phone.

2

Coupons content is strengthened by cashback offers which upsell into Groupon Bucks - leading the user back to Groupon.

3

Rather than the Genie extension which only works on desktop, this works on mobile - where our customers shop! 61% of our traffic is mobile.

4

As an extension of the app, acquisition is supercharged using the existing app base - 8-10M monthly active users, no separate download needed.

5

Integrating data from users' off-Groupon shopping experience into their Groupon profile enhances opportunity for personalisation.

The Groupon Bucks Flywheel

Affiliate coupons and core deals reinforce each other when both live in the app.

Cross-Pollination Flywheel

User needs a Nike code → Opens Groupon app → Sees daily deal / local offer → Books a spa deal → Earns Bucks in wallet → Comes back for next code

Every coupon lookup is an exposure to a core deal. Every core deal visit is an exposure to coupons. The wallet connects both.

Groupon Bucks - The Connective Tissue

The connective tissue for everything. A visible, growing balance ("You've earned $14.20 this month") that accumulates from coupon use, cashback, referrals, and code verifications. Users check their balance even when not shopping. This is what makes the flywheel spin.

The Numbers

The Opportunity

  • 8-10M US app MAU (Tableau, Mar 25 - Apr 7) - no new acquisition needed
  • 61% of coupons traffic is mobile (BQ) - Genie can't reach them, Safari can
  • ~50% US mobile Safari share (StatCounter)
  • $9.81/user/yr potential with Bucks (Genie LTV model, $6 base + $3.81 Bucks uplift)

Current Coupons Performance (declining)

  • ~30M US sessions/yr (BQ, 2025) - down 17% YoY from 35.8M (2024)
  • $12.6M US commission (BQ, 2025) - tracking to ~$8.8M in 2026
  • $0.43 revenue per session - we capture a fraction of user intent

Genie Desktop - Proof the Model Works

  • 981 installs, $6/user/yr (CJ actuals, 23 weeks)
  • 14% landing page to install conversion rate
  • 34 MD rewards integration = 26-59x install growth (8-18K/mo projected)
  • Popup CTR: 1.45% (best performing promotional asset)

Revenue Potential

  • Safari extension: $4.4-16.2M/yr (10-30% activation of iOS app base)
  • Registration + Genie (without Safari): $4-7M/yr
  • Combined: $8-25M/yr for ~40 MD engineering
  • "Even without Safari, registration + Genie alone deliver a 33% uplift on current revenue."

Unknowns

  • iOS App Store approval for bundled Safari extension (2-week spike to validate)
  • Extension activation rate (Honey gets ~30%, we estimate 10-30%)
  • iOS share of app users (estimated ~55%, need confirmed data from Radomir/BI)

Competitor Proof

  • Honey (PayPal app), Rakuten, Capital One Shopping all ship Safari extensions
  • Honey in crisis: lost 8M users, class action lawsuit - market window open
  • Capital One Shopping: #2 free Safari app, 4.9 stars, 960K reviews
  • Full research: 7 competitors analyzed

Safari Extension + Cashback/Bucks + Coupons in the App - Why We Need All Three

Rather than a standalone extension with just coupons content, we believe the real value would be based on the extension + cashback/Bucks + coupons account/app integration.

Safari Extension = Reach

Extends Groupon into every shopping session on mobile Safari. The distribution channel. Works on the 61% of traffic that desktop Genie can't reach. Every Groupon app install becomes a Safari extension install for free.

Cashback / Groupon Bucks = Value Creation

The cashback element gives us a way of creating valuable content for the user without being reliant on codes issued by merchants. Most importantly, cashback also opens the doorway to an upsell into Groupon Bucks - the mechanism that pulls users back into the app to spend on local deals.

Coupons Integration into the App = Personalisation

Coupons integration into the account opens the way for more personalisation and messaging - allow customers to follow their favourite brands for alerts when coupons/cashback offers are available. Data from off-Groupon shopping enriches the user profile.

A standalone coupons extension is limited. All three together create an ecosystem where every shopping session feeds back into Groupon.

The Coupons Space - Supporting Initiatives

Lead with Safari - then support with these. Our organic coupons traffic is declining (30M sessions, -17% YoY), but these initiatives both stand on their own and feed the Safari extension strategy:

User Registration + Rewards

  • Convert anonymous coupons visitors into registered Groupon users
  • $3.83 per registration (Behdad/BI, 17M+ sample, 12% convert to purchasers)
  • Needs Groupon auth integration (Phase 1: 2-3 weeks for "Sign in with Groupon")
  • Incentive: gift cards or rewards gating (prior test showed +1,247% sign-up uplift - Rebecca's ROI model)
  • Revenue: $2.1-4.6M/yr

Genie Desktop + Rewards

  • Already live: 981 installs, $6/user/yr validated
  • 34 MD rewards integration = 26-59x install growth
  • Proves the extension model that Safari inherits
  • Revenue: scale dependent, currently bottlenecked on acquisition

Cashback

  • Already in planning - builds on Groupon Bucks infrastructure being developed for Safari
  • Evolved rewards for repeat purchases
  • % back on every purchase as Bucks, redeemable on core deals

Every registered coupons user becomes a potential Safari extension user. These paths feed the primary strategy.
