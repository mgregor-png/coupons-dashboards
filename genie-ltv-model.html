The Investment Question

BQ MAU more than doubled in 2 months (266 -> 618) with no auto-apply, no rewards. That is genuine product-market signal. Per-MAU run-rate of $42.33/yr at 618 MAU = $26K/yr from CJ alone. Reward ROI thesis remains in Genie's favour (9.6x vs Groupon 5.3x). The case for accelerating auto-apply + rewards is now stronger, not weaker.

Decision gate (revised): If BQ MAU continues +10% MoM through Q2, ship rewards. If MAU growth stalls, investigate before adding feature complexity.

Benchmark: Honey (pre-acquisition) reported ~60% 12-month retention -> ~2.5x multiplier. Genie's MAU growth rate is currently consistent with (or ahead of) early-Honey trajectory adjusting for scale.
