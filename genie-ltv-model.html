The Investment Question

1,000+ installed users generating $26K/yr commission with no auto-apply, no rewards. That is $25.93/install/yr - 4.3x what v3 estimated. The retention gap between current product (1.79x BQ-observed) and industry benchmark (2.0-2.4x) is the value that shipping auto-apply + rewards unlocks. Rewards ROI now favours Genie (9.6x) over the wider Groupon marketplace (5.3x).

Decision gate: If 30-day MAU stabilises above 800 within 90 days of auto-apply launch, continue investment. If MAU continues the 1,377 -> 771 decline trajectory after feature completion, reassess.

Benchmark: Honey (pre-acquisition) reported ~60% 12-month retention -> ~2.5x multiplier. Auto-apply creates a "set and forget" habit loop that dramatically improves retention vs. active-use extensions.
